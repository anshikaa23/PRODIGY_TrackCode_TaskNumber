Astitva Fest

About Astitva

Astitva Fest is the largest cultural cum tech fest in Prayagraj, organized by United Institute of Technology, Prayagraj.

  • It is a platform for students to showcase their talents and skills.
  • It provides an opportunity for students to interact with industry experts and learn from them.
  • It is a celebration of art, culture, and technology.
  • Astitva features a range of performances, including music, dance, theater, and poetry, showcasing the talents of students from various colleges and universities.
  • The festival celebrates the diversity of Indian culture through its vibrant cultural events, including traditional music and dance performances, fashion shows, and food festivals.
  • Astitva provides a platform for students to connect with like-minded individuals, make new friends, and build relationships that can last a lifetime.
